Every Picture Experiments

Experiment 1: Zoom In While Hovering

Simulate zooming in on a specific part of the image to look for the answer and getting it right.

Experiment 2: Cropping an Image

Cropping to zoom in really far into an image. Zoom in far enough to see the pixels, then upscaling the image so it is undefined and slightly blurry.

Experiment 3: Emoji Overlay

When hovering over an incorrect section, an emoji will pop up to indicate you should look elsewhere.
